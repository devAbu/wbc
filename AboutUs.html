About Us


A WBC online banking system is a management information system for online transactions such as sending and receiving money or selling and buying things that you find on internet. We in WBC will propose the development of easier managing of your money which will provide capabilities for you to easily buy/sell items,goods etc. and being able to review your bank account at any time of the day and besides that you can send or receive money online.

WBC is the safer, easier way to pay and get paid online. The service allows anyone to pay in any way they prefer, including through credit cards, bank accounts or account balances, without sharing financial information.
